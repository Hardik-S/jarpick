# JarPick Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: May 10, 2026

JarPick stores your jars, choices, and pick history on this device. JarPick does not require an account.

JarPick does not use a backend, AI, push notifications, external payments, subscriptions, or sensitive permissions. JarPick does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, or calendar permissions.

If ads are enabled, Google Mobile Ads may collect data, including advertising identifiers where available, device information, app interactions, diagnostics, analytics signals, and fraud prevention signals. Premium removes ads.

JarPick uses Google Play Billing for the one-time `remove_ads_premium` product. Purchases are handled by Google Play.

You can delete local JarPick data from within app settings, through Android app storage settings, or by uninstalling the app.
