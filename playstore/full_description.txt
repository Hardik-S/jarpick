JarPick is a simple random decision maker for everyday choices.

Create jars for dinner ideas, movies, chores, study tasks, games, date nights, weekend plans, or anything else. Add your options, tap to pick, and let the jar choose one.

JarPick is built for small, low-stakes decisions that waste too much time.

Features:
• Create custom choice jars
• Add your own options
• Tap to pick a random result
• Avoid immediate repeats
• View recent pick history
• Use starter jars for dinner, chores, movies, study, and weekends
• Works without an account
• Stores your jars locally on your device
• Optional $2.99 premium upgrade removes ads and unlocks more features

Premium unlocks:
• No ads
• Unlimited jars
• Unlimited choices
• Weighted picks
• No-repeat mode
• Elimination mode
• Premium themes
• Full pick history

JarPick is not for gambling, lotteries, raffles, or prize drawings. It is a lightweight decision helper for everyday choices.
