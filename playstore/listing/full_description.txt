JarPick helps you make low-stakes decisions without overthinking.

Create jars for meals, chores, games, date ideas, study breaks, or any repeating choice. Add options once, then let JarPick draw one when you need a quick answer.

What JarPick does:
- Build multiple local choice jars
- Add, edit, and remove options
- Randomly pick from a jar
- Keep simple everyday decisions lightweight
- Offer an optional remove-ads upgrade

JarPick is designed for casual personal use. It does not make medical, financial, legal, safety, or other high-stakes recommendations.
