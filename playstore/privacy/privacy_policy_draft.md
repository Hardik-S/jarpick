# JarPick Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: May 10, 2026

JarPick is an Android app for creating local jars of choices and randomly selecting an option.

## Data We Store

JarPick stores the jars and options you create on your device. The MVP is designed for local use and does not require an account.

## Purchases

JarPick may offer an optional one-time purchase called `remove_ads_premium`. Purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. JarPick receives purchase status from Google Play so it can unlock the premium ad-free entitlement.

## Ads

JarPick may show banner ads through Google AdMob to non-premium users on allowed screens. AdMob may collect data according to Google's advertising policies and the user's device settings.

## Data Sharing

JarPick does not sell personal information. Google Play Billing and Google AdMob may process data needed to provide purchases and ads.

## Children

JarPick is intended as a general utility. Do not enter sensitive personal information into jar names or options.
