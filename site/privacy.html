JarPick

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

JarPick is an Android app for creating jars of everyday choices and randomly selecting one option. The app is designed for local, low-stakes decision making.

Data Stored On Your Device

JarPick stores your jars, choices, settings, and pick history on your device. JarPick does not require an account and does not send your jar contents to a JarPick backend.

Ads

JarPick may show banner ads to free users through Google AdMob. Google Mobile Ads may collect data such as advertising identifiers, device information, app interactions, diagnostics, and fraud-prevention signals according to Google's advertising policies and your device settings.

Purchases

JarPick may offer a one-time premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. Google Play processes purchases and payment information. JarPick receives purchase status from Google Play so the app can unlock premium features and remove ads.

Data Sharing

JarPick does not sell personal information. Google AdMob and Google Play Billing may process data needed to provide ads, purchases, analytics, security, and fraud prevention.

Children

JarPick is a general-audience utility and is not directed to children. Do not enter sensitive personal information into jar names or choices.

Deleting Data

You can delete local JarPick data from within the app settings, through Android app storage settings, or by uninstalling the app.
