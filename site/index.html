Android utility

JarPick

Random choice jars for everyday, low-stakes decisions.

Privacy policy Support

What JarPick does

Create jars for dinner ideas, chores, movies, games, study tasks, weekend plans, or any other small decision. Add options, tap to pick, and keep the history on your device.

Launch status

JarPick stores jars locally, does not require an account, and uses Google Play Billing for the optional premium upgrade. Free users may see banner ads served through Google AdMob.
